Travel and Visa

Travel and Visa norms help. Accomodation details will be updated soon.

Conference / Business Visa

  • Valid for 6 months or 1 year with multiple entries

Valid for six month or one/more years with multiple entries. However, the period of stay in India (for each visit) under this category is limited to six months only. A letter from the sponsoring organization indicating nature of applicant’s business, probable duration of stay, validity of visa, places and organizations to be visited and also a guarantee to meet maintenance expenses etc. should accompany the application. Business visa valid for ten years with multiple entries is available to foreign businessmen who have set up or intend to set up joint ventures in India.

Travel

Chennai is well connected by air, rail and road. Direct flights are available from various cities across the globe. Rail network is also very strong with Chennai Central and Chennai Egmore serving as a hub for trains from cities and towns across India. VIT campus is well served by local trains, buses and cabs from both airport and train stations.

Participants from abroad need a valid visa to enter the country. Participants can write a mail to the organizers for suitable letter for visa processing.
