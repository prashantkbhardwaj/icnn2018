About the conference

It is a great pleasure to announce that 5th International Conference of Nano-materials and Nano-composites (ICNN) will be held at VIT University, Chennai during 08-10 February, 2018. It is very exciting to see nano-images of materials and their possible immense applications in the daily science column of newspapers. Every month numerous research papers are being published on potential nano-materials and their associated applications for the benefit of the society. Looking at the diversified research findings, it appears that in almost all the fields of materials research, nanostructures are bringing innovation and technological applications. 5th ICNN, therefore seeks a conglomeration of researchers and experts from academia and industries across the globe, to exchange ideas and intricacies of technological advances in nano-world. Experimental and theoretical aspects on nanoparticles/composites, CNT, graphene and other nanostructures will be addressed in this forum. Special emphasis will be given to interdisciplinary areas of health, energy and environment—their challenges and success stories.

We invite you to participate in this conference at VIT University Chennai and exchange ideas and present your findings. It is believed that everyone will be benefited through interaction with experts from various fields leading to enriched research output. Definitely, several research collaborations will be established by the researchers of mutual interest through this conference platform. Awards will be given to outstanding research students for their oral/poster presentations. Special awards will also be announced for the researchers and faculty members for their exceptional research contributions. International and national scientific jurists will decide the selection process.

This conference is being organized in association with International Institute for Carbon Neutral Energy, Kyushu University, Japan. The Industry partner for this conference is Dr. Reddy’s Lab, Hyderabad, India.

History of the conference

The Previous edition of the conference, Fourth International Conference on Nanostructured Materials and Nanocomposites (ICNM 2017) was held between February 10-12, 2017 at Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam, Kerala, India.
